Biography

Chaolong Ying is a postgraduate student at Xidian University under the supervision of Prof. Jing Liu. He obtained his bachelor's degree in 2019 at Xidian University and got postgraduate recommendation. His research goal is to solve modeling, optimization and learning problems on real-world networks using graph convolutional network and evolutionary algorithm.
